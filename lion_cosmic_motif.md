# Lion Cosmic Motif Notes

Based on the emoji sequence (`🦁🦋🙇🏽‍♂️😉🐉🌏`) and attached images, this motif blends:

- **Lion archetype**: strength, guardianship, centered calm.
- **Butterfly + bowing gesture**: transformation with humility.
- **Wink**: confidence with playfulness.
- **Dragon + Earth**: mythic scale and planetary stewardship.

## Visual Throughline

The image set converges on a recurring style language:

1. **Central lion subject** in seated, frontal pose.
2. **Dark, cinematic backgrounds** with high contrast.
3. **Glowing eyes** as focal accent.
4. **Cosmic context** (stars/planets/comets) in the brighter variants.
5. **Sculptural rendering** that reads as a statue or icon.

## Prompt Seed (Reusable)

> A regal lion statue centered in frame, cinematic low-key lighting, subtle teal bioluminescent accents, deep space backdrop with sparse stars and distant planets, symmetrical composition, high detail, mystical but calm mood, ultra-clean dark palette.

## Optional Variants

- **Guardian mode**: colder tones, brighter eye glow, minimal planets.
- **Mythic mode**: add dragon constellation silhouettes and comet trails.
- **Earthkeeper mode**: include a softly lit Earth-like horizon in background haze.
